MY STORY

I was born 20.5.1989 in a small Croatian town of Rijeka. Rijeka is know for its open and international way of thinking, Ucka mountain, Tourism and the Croatias industry heart, the Shipyard 3.Maj. After electrical high school and university I decided to get experience from the maritime industry. In the three years that followed I moved from being a electrical cadet to electrotechnical officer. On 2016 I decided to return to a shore based job and the perfect place to improve and share my accuired skills was industrial automation.

I strive to be better than the day before not only in my work but in personal life, sports and experience. Going forward is the only way I know. There is nothing better that I like than to solve an really complex problem, to be the guy who gets all things done. There is no problem that cannot be fixed. The only thing you have to do is look at it and the solution will pop into your head, eventually.

I have also many hobbies for example: Travelling is the best way. Meeting new cultures and people opens your mind to new possibilities. Thats why I tend to travel as much as I can.See the map HERE. From cycling, running, gymnastics to the dynamic Street Workout, anything goes just to keep healthy and happy. More of that on the Street Workout page and Youtube channel
